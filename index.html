Short Bio

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), ranked 3rd among my peers with a GPA of 3.89/4.00. My research interests lie at the intersection of trustworthy machine learning and deep learning, with a particular focus on fine-tuning foundation models and machine unlearning.

Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at Hong Kong Baptist University under the supervision of Dr. Qizhou Wang and Prof. Bo Han, where I am developing novel methodologies for LLM unlearning. I have also contributed to research in multi-label object detection and vision-language models through collaborations with leading researchers in the field.

My academic journey has been enriched by participation in Mathematics and Computer Olympiads, where I spent 2 years focusing on algorithms and problem-solving. This foundation has helped me excel in competitive programming, including achieving 10th place in the 46th ICPC West Asia Regional Contest.
