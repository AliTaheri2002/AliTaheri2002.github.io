Short Bio

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), ranked 3rd among my peers with a GPA of 3.89/4.00. I worked as a Research Assistant at the Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) Group, Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, under the supervision of Dr. Qizhou Wang and Prof. Bo Han. I have also worked on multi-label object detection and vision-language models under the supervision of Prof. Abdolreza Mirzaei and Prof. Oliver Schulte from SFU, contributing to the ROAD-R 2023 NeurIPS challenge. Recently, I joined the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS) for summer internship.

I am currently researching challenges related to unlearning and fine-tuning in foundation models. My research interests also include computer vision, deep learning, and trustworthy machine learning.
